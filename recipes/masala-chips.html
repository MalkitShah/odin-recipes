Masala Chips

Description

Crispy golden fried potato chips with flavourful Indian style masala sauce, which has a spicy, tangy, curry taste.

Ingredients

  • Potato Chips
  • Masala Sauce
  • Curry powder
  • Chilli
  • Garnish

Steps:

  1. Fry or bake the chips until crispy golden coloured
  2. In a large non-stick wok/pan, saute the onion and garlic with low-medium heat until light brown.
  3. Next add the spices, turmeric, curry powder, black pepper, and dried chilli flakes. Saute for few seconds until fragrant.
  4. Stir in tomato ketchup, salt, and water. Cook until the sauce starts to get thick.
  5. When the masala sauce is ready, add the fried potato chips and tossed well to combine everything evenly.
  6. Turn off the heat and sprinkle chopped coriander and chilli slices. Serve immediately while hot.
