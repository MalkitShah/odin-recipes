Chicken Lollipop

Description

Chicken lollipop is a popular indo-chinese appetizer where a frenched chicken drumette is marinated and then batter fried or baked until crisp

Ingredients

  • Chicken drummettes
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Red chilli powder
  • Cornstarch

Steps:

  1. Remove skin and fats off the drummettes
  2. Add chilli powder, ginger garlic and pepper powder. Toss gently to coat
  3. Mix cornflour with salt. Add it all to a bowl and mix
